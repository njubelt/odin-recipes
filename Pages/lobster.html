Fresh, Hard-to-Crack Lobster (from S08E3)

The hands of a man. It's like a creature out of Greek mythology. I mean, she was like part woman, part horrible beast.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp sea salt
  • 4 (6 oz) lobster tails
  • 1/2 cup butter, melted

Directions

Pour about 1 inch of water in the bottom of a large pot and bring to a boil. Add the salt and place a steamer insert inside the pot so that it is just above the water level. Put the lobster tails on the rack and cover the pot. Cover and steam for 8 minutes. And don't peek! Serve with melted butter.

Nutrition Facts

Per serving:356 calories; protein 32.2g; carbohydrates 0.9g; fat 24.5g; cholesterol 222.5mg; sodium 1986.6mg.
